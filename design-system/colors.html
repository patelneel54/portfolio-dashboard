Color

The dashboard runs in a single dark theme tuned for late-night glances at quotes. Surfaces stack from near-black up through three subtle elevations. Color earns its place — every accent encodes a meaning: account type, asset class, or financial direction.

Surfaces

Four-step stack. The page sits on --bg, primary cards on --card, inputs and popovers on --elevated. Hairline borders, never shadows, separate same-tier surfaces.

Text

Three weights of legibility. Body type holds at --text; supporting metadata falls to --text-muted; everything decorative or low-priority sits on --text-dim.
