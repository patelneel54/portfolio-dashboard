Type

Two faces, two jobs. DM Sans handles UI chrome, headings, and prose. JetBrains Mono renders every number — prices, percentages, dates, share counts — with tabular figures so columns align without effort.

Faces

Both ship from Google Fonts. The split is deliberate: humans read prose in proportional type but parse numbers faster in monospace, especially when scanning a holdings table.
