Brand & Iconography

The product is named Portfolio Command Center, "Portfolio CC" on the home screen. The mark — a four-bar chart with a connecting trend line — captures the dual identity: holdings (the bars) and performance (the line).

Portfolio Command Center
Stock portfolio tracker & analyzer · iOS PWA

The icon stays on a #0a0e17 rounded square — the only place an off-token color is allowed, because Apple's home-screen mask demands a dense backplate. Bars run blue → green → indigo → amber; the trend line sweeps from lower-left to upper-right in #e2e8f0.

Tone is direct, confident, never financial-bro. We say "Brokerage" not "Wealth". "Drift" not "Asset Allocation Variance". Numbers do the bragging — adjectives stay quiet.

App icon · sizes

Three packaged sizes plus the SVG master. The maskable purpose tells iOS and Android that the icon's safe zone is centered with a 12% padding, so no element is clipped by the home-screen mask.

Iconography

Linear icons, 24×24 viewBox, 2 px stroke, round caps and joins. Hand-rolled in BottomTabBar as path arrays — no icon library. Always inherits currentColor: one icon per concept; never paired with an emoji or filled glyph.

Voice

The product is for one user — its owner. Copy is terse, reads like a fluent operator, and never apologizes. Numbers and ticker symbols carry the weight; prose stays out of the way.

Say this
  • "Updated 2m ago"
  • "3 alerts triggered"
  • "Get alerts when stocks hit price targets"
  • "+14.2% return"
  • "24 positions across 3 accounts"
  • "Prices refresh daily at market close"
Not this
  • "Your data was last synchronized two minutes ago"
  • "Attention: 3 of your alert subscriptions have triggered"
  • "Stay informed about your investments with notifications!"
  • "You are up an impressive 14.2%"
  • "You currently hold 24 different investment products"
  • "Quotes are subject to delay; please check back later"
